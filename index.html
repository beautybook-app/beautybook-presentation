How it works

Simple for everyone.

One app, two experiences: clients book, salons manage — all in seconds.

Client Side Mobile app
1
Discover salons
Browse nearby salons, filter by service (haircut, manicure, massage…), view photos, reviews, services, employees and hours.
2
Pick a time slot
Select a service, choose a staff member, and book the available slot that works — in under 30 seconds.
3
Instant confirmation
If the slot is free, the booking is confirmed immediately — no waiting, no phone call. The appointment appears directly in their history.
4
Flexible pending window
Not every booking needs to be instant. Salons can mark certain slots as pending — shown in orange in the calendar. When a client books one, the slot is temporarily blocked for 20 minutes so no one else can take it. Meanwhile, the staff member receives a notification and can confirm or decline at their own pace, without being interrupted mid-service. This gives professionals full control over complex schedules, back-to-back appointments, and last-minute requests — all without losing the booking.

Salon Side Owner dashboard
1
Automatic bookings
When a slot is free, the booking is confirmed automatically — no action needed. Your schedule fills itself.
2
Handle edge cases
For tight slots, the request appears as pending. One tap to confirm or decline — competing requests are cancelled automatically.
3
Complete dashboard
View all your bookings by day. Each employee sees their own schedule. Zero risk of double-booking, ever.
4
Full service catalogue, your way
Create your services with a name, price, duration, category and description. Organise them into as many service groups as you need — e.g. "Haircuts", "Colour", "Treatments". Each service can be assigned to specific staff members, so clients only see what each professional actually offers. No limits, no templates — your catalogue reflects exactly how your salon works.
